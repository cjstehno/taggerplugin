Resource Tagging Plug-in

Overview

The Resource Tagging plug-in add the ability to associate semantic tags to one or more resources in the workspace. These tags may then be used to provide additional grouping and filtering options or simply as additional meta labeling information.

Contact

You can contact the developer(s) of this plugin online at one of the project's web sites: http://taggerplugin.sourceforge.net or http://sourceforge.net/projects/taggerplugin
